    • About BR International School

    We are an institution which provide quality education to its children . We have launched major initiative to promote the teaching of science, computer and information technology in school. We do not merely prepare the students to pass examination and get jobs.We take responsibility to make them ready for life. Our motive is to build the character of the students.Our commitment is to nurture the inborn talents of the children with high sense of the discipline.We understand the need of the students.

    Our Mission

    Our Mission is to prepares students to understand, contribute to, and succeed in a rapidly changing society and also generating practical and theoretical knowledge that enables people to better understand our world and improve conditions for local and global communities.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to productive learning environment, in which student can communicate effectively, think critically, solve problems and technologically literate through a varity of extra -curricular activities.The entire purpose of education is not to restrict itself to imparting bookish knowledge only but inculcate humanitarian values like wisdom, compassion, courage, humility, integrity and reliability in a student.
