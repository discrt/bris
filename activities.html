    • Activities

    Yoga

    Yoga has been an integral part of Health and Physical Education that has been a compulsory subject up to the our school. These trainings are provided during school hours making it easy and fun for all.

    Music

    We offer classes for Classical ,Hindustani Folk, Bhajan ,Rabindra Sangeet, Thumri, Dadra, Ghazal, Qawwali, Chaiti ,Kajari, Sufi. These trainings are provided during school hours making it easy and fun for all kinds of events

    Art & Craft

    The School offers classes for Decorative Arts, Applied Art, Crafting with specializations in Texttiles, Paper Craft, Pottery and Glass Crafts. Basket weaving, Doll-making, Enamelling, Floral Design, Jewellery-making, Model-making, Toy-making.

    Dance

    BR INTERNATIONAL is one of the leaders in providing dance training to students with cultural societies and expertise of our dance trainers. offer Western and Indian Traditional Dances along with Aerobics and all other contemporary forms.

    Singing

    Our vision is to make learning of all styles of Indian music easy, accessible, and exciting to children. Students are trained for various programmes, school functions, stage shows, Television Shows.

    Cricket

    BR INTERNATIONAL gives children the opportunity to develop their cricket skills with a fun, safe and sociable environment. Our training cater for boys and girls, of all abilities, from ages six to 18.

    Football

    We gives children the opportunity to develop their football skills with a fun, safe and sociable environment. Our training caters to the needs of boys and girls, of all abilities.

    Kabaddi

    BR INTERNATIONAL gives children the opportunity to develop their kabaddi skills with a fun, safe and sociable environment. Our training cater for boys and girls, of all abilities, from ages six to 18.
